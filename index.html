About

TWICE

South Korean girl group that is consisting of nine members.
The group officially debut on October 20, 2016, under JYP Entertainment,
with their debut song Like Ooh-Ahh.
Their first mini-album was The Story Begins
, followed by Page Two, TWICEcoaster: LANE 1,
and TWICEcoaster: LANE 2.
